About

We are driven by community. With over a decade in the coffee industry, we know that the choices we make have a big impact both locally and at origin. From sourcing and roasting to packaging and delivering, we like to make the work we do count in a big way, both in our community and the far away farmers who grow our amazing coffees. We really owe it all to them.

At Happy Cup, we’re passionate about doing our part to support the local community. We do this by donating a portion of sales from a rotating coffee to local charities in Melbourne, or, with a focus on underrepresented individuals and communities.
